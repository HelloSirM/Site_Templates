About SafeKids

SafeKids is a comprehensive disaster preparedness education platform designed specifically for students from Year 1 through Year 10.

Our mission is to make emergency preparedness accessible, engaging, and developmentally appropriate for all school-aged children.

Developed in partnership with emergency responders, educators, and child psychologists, our materials meet national safety education standards.
